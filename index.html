Privacy Policy for KuNect

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to KuNect ("we," "our," or "us"). KuNect is a social networking application designed exclusively for students of Kathmandu University. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using KuNect, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address (@student.ku.edu.np)
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Academic information (department, year)

Profile Information:

  • Profile picture
  • Bio/description
  • Interests and hobbies

Content You Create:

  • Posts and comments
  • Anonymous gossip posts (with anonymous username aliases)
  • Likes and reactions
  • Pokes sent and received
  • Reports and feedback

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • IP address

Usage Information:

  • Features you use
  • Actions you take (posts, comments, likes, pokes)
  • Time and duration of activities
  • Search queries

Notification Information:

  • Push notification tokens (for sending notifications)
  • Notification preferences

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not access your location (unless you enable it for future features)
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Provide and Improve Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable you to create posts, comments, and interact with other students
  • Show you relevant content and recommendations
  • Facilitate the poke feature and leaderboards
  • Send you notifications about activity on your account

3.2 Safety and Security

  • Verify you are a Kathmandu University student
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Respond to legal requests

3.3 Communication

  • Send you service-related announcements
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Notify you about new features (you can opt-out)

3.4 Analytics and Improvement

  • Understand how users interact with KuNect
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Develop new features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

Public Information (visible to all KuNect users):

  • Username
  • Profile picture
  • Bio
  • Academic information (department, year)
  • Posts and comments (unless anonymous)
  • Poke statistics (weekly leaderboard)

Anonymous Content:

  • Gossip posts and comments are shown with randomly generated anonymous usernames
  • Your real identity is NOT revealed for anonymous content
  • We maintain the link between you and your anonymous content for moderation purposes only

Not Shared:

  • Your email address
  • Your password
  • Your date of birth
  • Private messages (when feature is added)

4.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who help us operate KuNect:

  • Cloud hosting providers (for data storage)
  • Push notification services (Expo Push Notifications)
  • Analytics services (for app performance monitoring)
  • Email services (for verification emails)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
  • Requests from law enforcement
  • Protection of our rights and safety
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activity

4.4 Business Transfers

If KuNect is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.5 With Your Consent

We will share your information for other purposes only with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

5.2 Deleted Accounts

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information are deleted within 30 days
  • Posts and comments may be retained in anonymized form for community continuity
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days

5.3 Legal Obligations

We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., preventing abuse, resolving disputes).

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your information through:

  • Profile settings in the app
  • Account settings
  • Contacting us at privacy@kunect.app

6.2 Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time through app settings. This will remove your personal information within 30 days.

6.3 Communication Preferences

You can control:

  • Push notifications (in app settings or device settings)
  • Email notifications (in app settings)
  • Marketing communications (opt-out available)

6.4 Anonymous Content

You can choose to post anonymously using the gossip feature. We maintain the connection for moderation but do not reveal your identity to other users.

6.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting privacy@kunect.app

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication using JWT tokens
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

7.2 No Guarantee

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7.3 Your Responsibility

  • Keep your password confidential
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

8. Children's Privacy

KuNect is intended for university students aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

If you are under 18, do not use KuNect or provide any information.

If we discover we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Nepal, including:

  • Cloud servers (may be located internationally)
  • Service providers operating globally

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during international transfers.

10. Third-Party Services

10.1 Third-Party Links

KuNect may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies.

10.2 Third-Party Services We Use

  • Expo Push Notifications (for push notifications)
  • Cloud Storage Providers (for data hosting)
  • Email Services (for verification emails)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app

  • Displaying a prominent notice

Continued use of KuNect after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

The Last Updated Date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
